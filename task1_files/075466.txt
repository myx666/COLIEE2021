McKeown, J.
: The
Citizenship Act
does not provide power to extend the time period for filing an appeal under subsection 14(5). The Court has no jurisdiction to grant relief from late filing. See
Bakayoko, Re
(1993), 66 F.T.R. 133 (T.D.) and
Chen, Re
(1996), 122 F.T.R. 77 (T.D.). The
Rules
do not provide power to extend a time period stipulated by statute.
[2]
The motion by the applicant for an extension of time to file an appeal of the decision of Citizenship Judge Doreen Wicks dated December 7, 1999, is dismissed.
Motion dismissed.
